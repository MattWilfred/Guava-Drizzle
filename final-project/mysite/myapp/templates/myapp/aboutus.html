About us

Mission

The mission of the Crop Visualizer Web-Based Application is to provide an application that will help the farmers in the Central Luzon to visualize and forecast which crop is best to plant in their area. The web-based application will help to predict in which crop is best to plant based on soil and meteorological conditions. It also helps in predicting what soil and meteorlogical condition is best for the crop.

Vision

The Crop Visualizer Web-Based Application envision to deliver a quality, progressive and satisfying service to our target customers.
